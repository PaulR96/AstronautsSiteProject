About Us

Ape-X is an American aerospace manufacturer and space transportation services company headquartered in Turin, Italy. It was founded in 2002 by 4 young students (Paul, Luca, Wassim, Rodrigo) with the goal of reducing space transportation costs to enable the colonization of Mars. Ape-X manufactures the Falcon 9 and Falcon Heavy launch vehicles, several rocket engines, Dragon cargo and crew spacecraft and Starlink satellites.
