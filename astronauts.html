ACTIVE ASTRONAUTS

Rick Sanchez

Code name: S-909

Born: 28/12/1968

Nationality: US citizen

Years of service: 2009-present

Height: 6'7"

Mission: MS5H36 FDM7L0


Rick Sanchez is a United States Space Force colonel and APE-X astronaut. Sanchez was selected in June 2009 as a member of the NASA Astronaut Group 20. He made his first spaceflight as a Flight Engineer on Soyuz TMA-10M/Expedition 37, from September 2013 until March 2014.

Mick Silver

Code name: C-405

Born: 15/04/1965

Nationality: US citizen

Years of service: 2005-present

Height: 5'3"

Mission: GMTE74 ANEW78


Mick Silver is an American aeronautical engineer and an APE-X astronaut. His first spaceflight was as a Mission Specialist aboard Red-Mecha on 26 July 2005 for APE-X's first "return to flight" Space Shuttle mission after the Columbia disaster.
